Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a major international conference attended by thousands. Each year brings new innovations and conversations that shape the future of technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive, forward-thinking space where technology professionals, students, and entrepreneurs can connect, collaborate, and inspire one another. We strive to elevate ideas that drive progress and promote diversity in tech.
